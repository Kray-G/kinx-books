|   1   | Factor                   | Variable, Number, String, ...                                     |

|   9   | Comparison by Big/Small  | `<`, `>`, `>=`, `<=`                                              |
|  10   | Comparison by Equality   | `==`, `!=`                                                        |
|  11   | Bit AND                  | `&`                                                               |
|  12   | Bit XOR                  | `^`                                                               |
|  13   | Bit OR                   | `|`                                                               |
|  14   | Logical AND              | `&&`                                                              |
|  15   | Logical OR               | `||`                                                              |
|  16   | Ternary Expression       | ` ? : `, `function(){}`                                           |
|  17   | Assignment               | `=`, `+=`, `-=`, `*=`. `/=`. `%=`, `&=`, `|=`, `^=`, `&&=`, `||=` |
